About Navratri

Navratri is a nine-night festival dedicated to Goddess Durga. Celebrate the victory of good over evil with devotion, fasting, garba dances, and vibrant colors.

9 Divine Days

Day 1: Shailputri
Day 2: Brahmacharini
Day 3: Chandraghanta
Day 4: Kushmanda
Day 5: Skandamata
Day 6: Katyayani
Day 7: Kalaratri
Day 8: Mahagauri
Day 9: Siddhidatri
